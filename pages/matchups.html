Knockout Bracket Overview

After all eight league-phase matchdays are completed, the top teams move into the knockout rounds. Here’s a simple view of how the competition works.

Knockout Play-offs

Teams finishing 9th–24th in the league table play a two-leg tie to reach the round of 16. The top eight teams qualify directly.

Example tie: 9th vs 24th, 10th vs 23rd, and so on.

Round of 16 & Quarters

The eight league-phase seeds join the eight winners from the play-offs. All rounds except the final are home-and-away.

Aggregate score decides the winner; extra time & penalties if tied.

Semi-finals & Final

Two semi-final ties produce the finalists who meet in a single match at a neutral stadium.
