League Phase Overview

How the 36-team table works

Each club plays eight league-phase games against different opponents. Wins are worth three points and draws are worth one. The top eight teams go directly to the round of 16, while clubs in 9th–24th battle through knockout play-offs for the remaining spots.

Targets for top clubs

Giants like Real Madrid, Manchester City, Bayern, and Barcelona aim for strong finishes in the table to secure favorable seeding and avoid extra knockout rounds on the road to the final.
